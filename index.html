About Our Campaign 🐾

Pet-Friendly City is an initiative to build safe, inclusive, and compassionate urban environments where stray and domestic animals are treated with love and respect. We believe every pet deserves a place in our hearts and cities.

Join hands with fellow volunteers, animal lovers, and community leaders to make a difference—one paw at a time.
